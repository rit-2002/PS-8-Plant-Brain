AUDIT FINDING: AF-2026-002
Audit Type: Internal Quality & Compliance Audit
Plant: Vizag Steel Works
Auditor: Quality Assurance Cell, Corporate Office
Date: 2026-02-01

FINDING DESCRIPTION:
Audit identified a systemic gap: SCADA gas pressure monitoring (sensors
GPS-COB-* series) and the Permit-to-Work software operate as disconnected
systems with no automated cross-referencing. This was independently flagged
in Non-Conformance NC-2026-019 (raised during Inspection INS-2026-0341) and
is consistent with the contributing factor pattern described in Near-Miss
Report INC-2025-0118 and addressed procedurally (but not technically) in
SOP-COB-009 Revision 4.

RISK RATING: High. Three independent records (inspection, incident, and
procedure revision) point to the same unresolved technical gap across an
18-month period without remediation.

CORRECTIVE ACTION REQUIRED:
Plant Engineering to implement automated alerting linking SCADA gas pressure
readings to active Permit-to-Work status, with mandatory escalation per
SOP-COB-009 trigger criteria. Target closure: 2026-06-30.

REGULATORY REFERENCE: OISD-STD-113, Factory Act 1948 Section 87, DGMS
Circular 4/2025.
