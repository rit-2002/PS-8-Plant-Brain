SAFETY OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-COB-009
Title: Confined Space Entry - Coke Oven Battery Gas Mains
Plant: Vizag Steel Works
Revision: 4
Effective Date: 2025-09-01
Owner: Head of Safety, P. Krishnan

SCOPE:
This procedure applies to all confined space entries into coke oven gas main
tunnels, flue galleries, and sub-floor ducting across COB-1, COB-2, and COB-3.

PRE-ENTRY REQUIREMENTS:
1. Valid Permit-to-Work (PTW) issued by Shift In-Charge.
2. Gas test: O2 (19.5-23.5%), H2S (<10 ppm), CO (<35 ppm), confirmed by
   calibrated portable gas detector. Equipment Tag of detector logged on PTW.
3. No concurrent hot work permits within 15 metres of entry point - cross-check
   against active Hot Work Permit register before issuing PTW.
4. Standby person stationed at entry point for full duration.
5. Communication check (radio or signal line) established.

ESCALATION TRIGGER:
If gas pressure sensors (Tag Series: GPS-COB-*) show readings above 1.5x
baseline AND a confined space entry or hot work permit is active within the
same zone, the Shift In-Charge must suspend entry immediately and escalate to
Plant Safety Officer. This compound condition was identified as a contributing
factor in the January 2025 coke oven battery explosion (per The Wire
investigative report) and is now treated as a mandatory automatic stop-work
trigger under this revision.

REGULATORY BASIS: OISD-STD-113, Factory Act 1948 Section 36 (Confined Spaces),
DGMS Circular 4/2025.

RELATED DOCUMENTS: Incident Report INC-2025-0118, Work Order WO-4471 (fan
bearing failure pattern), Audit Finding AF-2026-002.
